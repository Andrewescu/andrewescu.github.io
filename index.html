About me

Curious, open-minded human aspiring to create software that can positively impact the world.

I'm fascinated by the abstract realms of math and physics and their relationship to both our world and the world of computer science. As a software engineer I endeavor to harness the powers of computers to not only better understand our complicated world but also to more effectively convey that understanding to everyone. Through the use of graphics rendering I seek to bring about an intuitive understanding of complicated systems via easy to understand visuals. I also seek to increase understanding by harnessing the analytical powers of machine learning technologies.
